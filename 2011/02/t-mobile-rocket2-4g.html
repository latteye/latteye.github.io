T-Mobile® Rocket 2.0 4G Laptop Stick Working in Ubuntu

2011年2月18日

This is a simple how to to help you T-Mobile Rocket 2.0 work in Linux.

vendor id and product id before usb_modeswitch 19d2:1201.
After usb_modeswitch 19d2:1203.

It has 2 ttyACM device and one usb ethernet device. The first ttyACM is modem dial port.

Here are the dail information for T-Mobile:
Dial number: 99**1#
Username: GPRS
Password: GPRS
APN: epc.tmobile.com

Here is the usb_modeswitch configure file:

`########################################################

DefaultVendor= 0x19d2
DefaultProduct= 0x1201

TargetVendor= 0x19d2
TargetProduct= 0x1203

CheckSuccess=20

MessageContent=”5553424392020000000000000000061B000000020000000000000000000000”`

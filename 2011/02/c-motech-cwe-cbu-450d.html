C-motech CWE-CBU-450D

2011年2月18日

Here is a simple Howto to Help you make C-motech 450D work in Linux.

vendor id and product id 16d8:700b. This card need usb_modeswitch but the vendor id and product id will not change after you modeswitch it.
But Ubuntu doesn’t has the driver. I tried both 2.6.32 and 2.6.35. We contact some engineer who can help me with the driver. This card work after load that driver.
Only 1 ttyUSB port show up.
